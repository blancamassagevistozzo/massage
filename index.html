Meet Blanca

Dedicated to the
Art of Healing.

Touch is an essential human need.

I’ve traveled to many countries, deepening my passion for the sacred art of touch and learning from different healing traditions along the way.

For the past 13 years, I’ve been dedicated to helping people feel more at ease, relaxed, and connected in their bodies. My work integrates knowledge from physiotherapy, Swedish massage, lymphatic drainage, Thai massage, yoga, deep tissue techniques, and tantric body awareness.

In every session, I bring not only technique, but also presence, care, and deep listening — meeting your body where it is and supporting its natural process of release and balance.
